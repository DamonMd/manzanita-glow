A Little About Us

If you're planning a wedding or other special event, you have a vision how you want it to look. Renting a venue is like an artist starting with a blank canvas, and then you get to paint the picture you want. Unique natural arches bring the ceremony site to life.

Your tabletop design is not complete without a centerpiece coordinated in your colors. Our rental centerpieces may accommodate floral designs if you wish, and with optional LED lighting add romantic glow to each table, and warm the entire room. Depending on your setup you may need room dividers to direct the flow of your guests from one area to another, or screens to hide service areas.

Meet the Owner

Emily has background in art, color, and interior design. She serves on the Board of Directors for the Reno/Tahoe Chapter of NACE - the National Association of Catering Executives.
